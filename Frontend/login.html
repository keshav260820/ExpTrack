How It Works

Three steps to your
perfect commute.

Getting started with Mitra Ride is effortless. Be on your way in minutes.

Step 01
🔍
Search Your Route

Enter your pickup and drop-off location. We'll instantly show you available rides matching your route and schedule.

Step 02
🤝
Match & Connect

Browse verified driver profiles, check ratings and reviews, then book your seat in just two taps.

Step 03
🚀
Ride & Save

Meet your carpool partner, enjoy the journey, and pay automatically. It's that simple — every day.

Features

Everything you need
in one place.

Mitra Ride is built around your safety, savings, and comfort.

🛡️

Verified Profiles

Every driver and passenger is identity-verified with Aadhaar or PAN. You always know exactly who you're riding with.

Smart Cost Splitting

Our algorithm automatically calculates the fairest fare split based on distance, fuel prices, and your route overlap.

Live Ride Tracking

Share your live location with family. Real-time GPS tracking and SOS button ensures you're always safe on the road.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get notified when your regular ride partner is available. Never miss a carpool opportunity on your daily commute again.
